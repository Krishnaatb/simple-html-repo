home is a warm and emotional Malayalam film that follows Oliver Twist, a gentle father struggling to understand modern technology while trying to reconnect with his sons. With a heartfelt performance by Indrans and relatable family moments, the movie beautifully explores love, communication, and the digital gap within households. It is a simple, feel-good drama that touches the heart.
